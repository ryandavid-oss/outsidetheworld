# Mar 19, 2014

![Post](Images/Instagram/11380135_1149516525074618_1875620265_n_17843195395024232.jpg)

Like the spring bursts from the ground
Unfurling from the ground
Faces of buildings, trees
All that is stone, inert
Here in these empty streets
It silently does assert
That we are just passing ghosts
Vapours of joy and hurt
