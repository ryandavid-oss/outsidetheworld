November 7th 1998



If I could describe a summer sunset

what words would I choose?



If I could make the world a better place

where would I start?



If I could go anywhere in the world right now

Why would I go there?



If I could choose just one person to spend eternity with

Who would I choose?



If I could have the three wishes of my heart granted

How would I do that?



And if I could choose a moment to begin to live

when would that be?

 

RyanDavid Burningham
