23 March 2009

08/08/2006

Tonight, The Boy and I went shopping.  For whom, for what occasion, and to what location will undoubtedly remain a secret for a time.  But while we were out, we made an important discovery:  Die-cast Cars toys. 

I've been looking for these for quite a while.  For whatever reason, they're in short supply; in fact, they've been so scarce that I figured them discontinued.  I bit the bullet and bought two of them; at this particular location, you tend to mortgage quite a bit of yourself whenever you make a purchase.

He was so pleased with these toys when he opened them up at home that he started to sing.  Yes, the little corporate-branded tike started singing the song from the Cars movie.  I didn't catch him during the moment, but I managed to coax a little bit out of him later.

It's not too big of a file, but I did have to compress it quite a bit for transfer purposes.  My apologies if it's a little grainy or if the sound level is too low.  I'm no video editor. 

He's just too great.  I almost hate to put him to bed.  His mom wouldn't necessarily like that he's up this late anyway, so regrettably, I must be going now.  I know, it's a short entry.  But where it lacks length, it's topped with character like the sugar on Sumner's oatmeal.  Ciao.s
